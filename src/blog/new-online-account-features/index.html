December 20, 2023 Updates

New Online Account Features

We're excited to announce significant enhancements to your Valley Car Credit online account! These new features are designed to give you more control, transparency, and convenience in managing your loan and tracking your credit journey.

Online Account New Features Technology

At Valley Car Credit, we are constantly striving to improve your experience. Based on your valuable feedback, we've rolled out a suite of new features to our online account portal. Here's what's new and how it benefits you:

1. Enhanced Payment Options

Making your car payments has never been easier or more flexible. We've expanded our payment functionalities to give you more control:

  • Multiple Payment Methods: Securely link and manage various payment methods, including bank accounts and debit cards.
  • Flexible Payment Scheduling: Set up one-time payments, recurring payments, or even schedule future payments to align with your paydays.
  • Payment History at a Glance: Easily view all your past payments, including dates, amounts, and methods used, for better record-keeping.

2. Real-Time Credit Score Tracking

One of the most requested features is here! You can now track your credit score directly within your online account. This powerful tool helps you:

  • Monitor Progress: See how your responsible payments are positively impacting your credit score over time.
  • Understand Factors: Get insights into the key factors influencing your score, helping you make informed financial decisions.
  • Receive Alerts: Opt-in for alerts on significant changes to your credit score, keeping you informed and proactive.

3. Personalized Credit Resources

We're committed to empowering you with the knowledge to improve your financial health. Our new resources section provides:

  • Tailored Articles: Access a library of articles and tips specifically curated to help you understand and improve your credit.
  • Interactive Tools: Utilize calculators and other tools to plan your financial future and see the impact of different strategies.
  • FAQs and Support: Quickly find answers to common questions and access direct support channels for personalized assistance.

4. Streamlined Document Access

No more searching for important documents! Your online account now provides easy access to:

  • Loan Documents: View and download your loan agreements, statements, and other essential paperwork anytime, anywhere.
  • Tax Forms: Conveniently access relevant tax documents when you need them.

5. Improved User Interface and Experience

We've redesigned the online portal with a cleaner, more intuitive interface. Navigating your account is now simpler and more efficient, whether you're on a desktop, tablet, or smartphone.

  • Mobile-Friendly Design: Enjoy a seamless experience across all your devices.
  • Quick Navigation: Find what you need faster with improved menus and search functionalities.

How to Access the New Features

Simply log in to your existing Valley Car Credit online account. All the new features will be automatically available to you. If you haven't set up your online account yet, it's quick and easy to do so!
